    ABOUT

    At Think Global we aim to embody the respect, acceptance and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists and believe in a world where everyone is free to have their own convictions while Accepting that others have the right to live in peace, regardless of the diversity of their beliefs, identity or way of life.

    We recognise that intolerance stems from ignorance and fear of difference cultures, nations or religions. Thus, or mission is to provide exchange programs that contribute to the understanding and learning of of societies with a different culture, religion, political or economic system.

    • A semester in Berlin

      Immerse yourself in the heart of Europe while studying at the prestigious 'Freie Universitat Berlin'.

      • Live in a hostel with 5 other exchange students
      • Organised cultural exchange programmes at local schools and businesses
      • Experience the centre of 20th century European history - a city divided in ideology
      • Excursions to countryside breweries for authentic German brewing and beer-tasting experience
      • Kick-start your future career with a German-centric focus

    • Away in Asia

      Experience a semester in the sun-soaked Phuket the tourists don't see.

      • Live in a hostel with 6 other exchange students at Prince of Songkla University
      • Organised cultural exchange programmes at local schools and businesses
      • Tropical water experiences, snorkeling, diving, boating
      • Excursions to Banana plantation - work a day in the life of a local
      • Equip yourself for close cultural ties to Thailand

    • Authentic Africa

      Spend a semester in Kenya studying at the University of Nairobi.

      • Live in a hostel with 4 other exchange students
      • Organised cultural exchange programmes at local schools and businesses
      • Volunteer work at food provincial food banks
      • Excursions to outback
      • See Africa for yourself

    • Brilliant Brazil

      Experience a semester in Rio studying at the famous 'Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro'.

      • Live in a hostel with 5 other exchange students
      • Organised cultural exchange programmes at local schools and businesses
      • Be a volunteer lifeguard at the Ipanema beach
      • Excursions to rural Brazilian villages
      • See the Olympic city

    • O Canada

      Study for a semester at McGill University and see the French side of Canada.

      • Live in a hostel in Stoneham, outside Quebec city with 5 other exchange students
      • Organised cultural exchange programmes at local schools and businesses
      • Work part-time at a local Maple Syrup factory
      • Excursions to rural Canadian villages
      • See the French side of Canada
