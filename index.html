ABOUT

At Think Global we aim to embody the respect, acceptance and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists and believe in a world where everyone is free to have their own convictions while Accepting that others have the right to live in peace, regardless of the diversity of their beliefs, identity or way of life.

We recognise that intolerance stems from ignorance and fear of difference cultures, nations or religions. Thus, or mission is to provide exchange programs that contribute to the understanding and learning of of societies with a different culture, religion, political or economic system.
